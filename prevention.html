How to Protect Yourself

Wear Mask

Always wear mask , when you visit market ares or you are going Outside for you work. Always wear a good quality mask

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hand 10 - 20 Times a Day. Or when you eat something first wash your Hand. Don't touch your face without washing you hand

Stay at Home

Don't Roam Outside , If you don't have any work. Stay Home Stay Safe . Go when work is Important

Use Senitizer

Use Senitizer , when you eat something outside. Don't touch you mouth, nose, or any face part without Wash you hand or Senitiz you Hand

Maintian Social Distancing

Maintain atleast 2 -3 meter Distancing to everyone , when you visit market. Don't shake you hand .
